DNA (Song Poster)

DNA is a dark, emotionally charged poster built around themes of identity, inheritance, and internal conflict. The design features a haunting close-up of an eye illuminated in deep crimson tones, with the title embedded directly into the sclera through branching vein-like typography. This surreal treatment symbolizes the idea that DNA is not just biological—it carries memory, pain, truth, and legacy. The distressed handwritten typography and intense red-black palette create a raw, unsettling atmosphere that aligns with aggressive, introspective musical storytelling. The overall composition evokes mystery, psychological depth, and a sense of confrontation with one’s inner self.

ANGER / CHAOS

Raw, explosive energy frozen in a single frame. It captures that moment when emotions stop being contained and start breaking everything around them. The feeling is uncontrollable, not just inside the body but spilling into the environment itself, distorting it. Nothing feels structured anymore—only impact, reaction, and the sharp edge of losing control.

FEAR / PARANOIA

A tense, unsettling moment where fear feels almost physical. The subject looks trapped inside his own thoughts, as if reality itself is closing in on him. Every shadow feels intentional, every sound feels too close, too aware. It’s the sensation of being watched even in absolute emptiness, where the mind starts turning against itself and safety feels like an illusion that’s slowly collapsing.

HAPPINESS / EUPHORIA

A loud burst of emotion that feels almost too intense to hold. Joy turns chaotic and wild, like happiness spilling out of control. The moment feels weightless, as if gravity briefly forgot its purpose. Everything becomes brighter, faster, louder—like the world is celebrating through the subject instead of around them, and restraint no longer exists.

EVOLVE

Evolve is a conceptual portrait poster that visually explores transformation, self-awareness, and personal growth. The composition features a softly lit human face illuminated by a butterfly-shaped projection, symbolizing metamorphosis and inner awakening. The grain-textured vintage treatment adds emotional depth and a nostalgic cinematic quality, while the refined serif typography reinforces themes of elegance and progression. The interplay between shadow and light reflects the journey of change—moving through uncertainty toward self-realization. The poster communicates evolution not as a sudden event, but as a gradual and deeply personal process.
